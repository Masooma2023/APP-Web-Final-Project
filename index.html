Foods of Afghanistan

Qabuli

Qabuli is the most famous Afghan food made with carrots, lentils, ground meat, rice, salt, black pepper, and other spices.p>

Bolani

Bolani is a type of Afghan stuffed bread made with dough filled with potatoes, onions, and other spices.

Ashak

Ashak is a type of Afghan soup made with sour ashak, mint, ground meat, chickpeas, lentils, and other additives.

Kichiri

Kichiri is a type of Afghan stew made with ground meat, lubiya (lentils), leek, split peas, red pepper, saffron, and other additives.

Nan Boti

Nan Boti is made from wheat flour, barley flour, and other additives such as salt, oil, and water.

Mantu

Mantu is a type of Afghan dumpling made with dough filled with ground meat, onions, and other spices.
